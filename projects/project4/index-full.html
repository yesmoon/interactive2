Track a Syrian Refugee, On Demand

How it Works

1. Download the app and sign up.

Enter your basic info to enable notifications on refugees you want to track.

2. Simply choose the state you want to track a refugee in.

You can also specify what gender and age group you would like to track.

3. Begin tracking!

Tap the blue dots on the map to view which refugee you are tracking. When desired, you can save a specific profile and receive notifications if the refugee is in your area.

About BombCam

BombCam is an app designed to help ease the tension created by the obtrusive flow of Syrian refugees into the United States.
BombCam allows you to track the Syrian Refugees entering the country and see their location in a city of your choosing. By allowing you to track the refugees, BombCam hopes to assure your physical safety in America.

You can track a Syrian refugee in 49 states.
