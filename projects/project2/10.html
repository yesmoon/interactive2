In the summer

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        I stretch out on the shore

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               And think of you



                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Had I told the sea

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           What I felt about you

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      It would have left its shores,



                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Its shells,

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Its fish,

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               And followed me
